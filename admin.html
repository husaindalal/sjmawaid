With the raza and dua of Al Dai al ajal al-Hayy al-Muqaddas Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin RA and Al Dai al ajal Syedna Mufaddal Saifuddin (TUS), mumineen of San Jose are blessed to receive the azeem nemat of Faiz-ul-Mawaid al-Burhaniya. We humbly arz laakho shukr na sajadaat to our beloved Aqa Moula, Aali Qadr Mufaddal Moula (TUS).

Under the tireless support and guidance of Janab Ammar bs Zakiuddin, San Jose Faiz-ul-Mawaid al-Burhaniya, steadily but surely inches closer towards its goal of delivering mawaid thaali to every mumin's home.

Parwardigar, apna Shafiq Bawa Aali Qadr Mufaddal Moula(TUS) ne sehat ane aafiyat na malabis ma Taa Roze Qayamat tak Baaqi ane Baaqi rakhe. Ameen.
